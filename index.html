About Spella

Spella Caffè specializes in traditional espresso, macchiato and cappuccino - from the careful selection, roasting and blending of coffee beans to the hand-pulled extraction of the ristretto. Spella Caffè offers fresh-brewed, single-origin coffees; handcrafted and made-to-order chai and teas; Italian sodas; and gelato, sorbetto, and affogato.

Our Coffee

Spella Caffè is currently roasting the following varieties of whole bean coffees:

Signature Espresso Blend: The lush textures of this classically Italian espresso carry chocolate and caramel tones―think crème brulée with candied orange and lime peel in the finish. Excellent for espresso, moka pot, and drip methods.

India Kattehollay: Direct from the family and farm from Karnataka State, our exclusive Peaberry is light to medium bodied with exotic spices and a snappy finish. This coffee truly shines with drip methods.

Ethiopia Sidamo Gr. 2: Vanilla, Nougat, with a Cabernet finish.

Ethiopia Chelfit Gr. 3: Spiced Dutch Cocoa, Strawberries and a dry finish.

Rossalina: Spiced Dutch Cocoa, Strawberries and a dry finish. An espresso blend named after our roaster! Thick, lush body, tons of crema, nutty, caramel tones with a healthy amount of licorice and anisette―versatile Rossalina is an afternoon pick-me-up or a wonderful end to a meal!
